1. Overview

The Colorado Top Bar v5.4

The Colorado Top Bar beehive is a CNC-routable, flat-packable, easy to ship and assemble hive made from sheet stock (typically exterior-grade plywood). It can be routed in under 40 minutes and assembled in 90 seconds.

The Colorado Top Bar hive was developed in Denver, Colorado and has been adapted and tested at Fab Lab Barcelona. It is a modern adaptation of the Kenyan Top Bar hive, which increases the internal space by adding top bars in a horizontal direction and has been designed to follow in a tradition of natural beekeeping methods.
