1. Overview

Hive History

The Colorado Top bar hive was developed in Denver, Colorado and has been adapted and tested at the Fab Lab Barcelona. It is a modern adaption of the Kenyan Top Bar Hive, this design increases the hive space by adding top bars in a horizontal direction and has been designed to follow in a tradition of natural bee keeping methods.
