Team

Jon Minchin

Co-Founder/Lead Designer

Jon is a co-founder and lead designer with the Open Source Beehives project. He is the coordinator of the Green Fab Lab at Valldaura, Spain and works at the intersection of digital fabrication with local material and living environments. You can find Jon on twitter @jonnyminch.

Tristan Copley-Smith

Co-Founder/Communications Director

Tristan is a co-founder and communications director with the Open Source Beehives project. Tristan is a documentary filmmaker and open source advocate, focused on disruptive technologies and their ability to transform our societies. You can find Tristan on twitter @trizcs.

Aaron Makaruk

Co-Founder/Project Management

Aaron is a social entrepreneur with over a decade of experience. He co-founded Youth On Record, Open Tech Collaborative, the Open Source Beehives project, and was formerly the Director of Development for Open Source Ecology.

Seneca Kristjonsdottir

Beekeeper Advisor

A beekeeper for the past ten years, Seneca has worked commercially with honeybees and earned a degree in bee husbandry and urban ecology. Founder of the Urban Pollination Project, she now works mentoring new beekeepers, building pollinator habitat in urban areas, and collaborating on projects like OSBH.

Garrett Burrg

Sensor Developer

Garrett is a hardware, firmware and python developer who's love of open source extends from hobby to political and environmental activism. Garrett works as a test development engineer for neutral.io

Chris Borke

Sensor Developer

Chris is a recent graduate from the University of Colorado in electrical and computer and computer science engineering. As a teenager, he became interested in beekeeping and maintains two honeybee hives in Boulder, Colorado. It is his goal to provide useful monitoring tools to aspiring beekeepers master and novice alike, as well as encourage interest in the art of keeping bees.

Patrick Beseda

Designer

Patrick is a designer & maker of digital and physical things. He's interested in architecture, digital craft, fabrication, and graphic representation. His focus is on designing solutions and authoring the processes to implement them; participating in the realization of projects whenever possible. Workflow and process oriented, tech-agnostic. He's always learning; new skills, new tools; new ways of thinking. (Website)
